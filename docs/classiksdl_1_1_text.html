◆ RenderMode

enum iksdl::Text::RenderMode
strong

Available rendering modes.

  • Solid is the fastest but has lowest quality
  • Blended is best quality but slowest

Shaded is also available, but handled through specific methods. It is best quality and quite fast, but has a background rectangle.
